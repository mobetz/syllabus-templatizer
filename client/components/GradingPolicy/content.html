Grading Policy

Grades serve two purposes: they allow instructors to communicate feedback to students on their mastery of course content, and they allow instructors to provide a final evaluation of student performance. I am sensitive to the fact that grades are a source of immense stress for many students. In order to reduce anxiety and promote an environment conducive to learning, I will drop the lowest lab grade received this semester. However, note that some labs build on the previous week, so this is not a license to completely skip an assignment knowing that it will be dropped. You may still be responsible for doing that work in order to complete the next week's lab.
