I am sensitive to the fact that grades are a source of immense stress for many students. In order to reduce anxiety and promote an environment conducive to learning, certain assignments are eligible for a 'make-up' lab. These make-up labs are alternate assignments that cover similar learning objectives to the original lab in a different way. Students may redeem a maximum of two make-up labs per semester; to attempt to redeem a make-up, attend office hours so that we may discuss assignment parameters.

Note: Make-up labs are not available for exams or project milestones.
